Justification of Design

Changes From Original Design

Given the amount of time allotted for my project, I believe that I did an excellent job of achieving my goals as previously set out in my Developing Ideas stage. Firstly, I decided to stay with my map in order to portray data because I believed that it would be a very interactive display of data. Furthermore, I changed the orientation of the page title, putting the title below the navigation bar. I also removed the country search due to time constraints. In the end, I believe my design is a simple and effective display of data that users can easily navigate through.
